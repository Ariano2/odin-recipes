Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is a refreshing and tangy beverage, a perfect thirst-quencher on hot summer days. Made by mixing freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and sugar, its zesty flavor provides a delightful balance of sweet and sour. The invigorating taste of lemonade uplifts spirits and offers a soothing respite from scorching temperatures, making it a beloved classic loved by people of all ages. Its versatility allows for various creative twists, like adding mint leaves or fresh berries, making each glass a unique and delightful experience.

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Water
  • Sugar

Preparation Procedure

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons into a pitcher.
  2. Add water and sugar to the pitcher.
  3. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved. Serve over ice and enjoy your refreshing homemade lemonade!
